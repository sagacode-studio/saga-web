# Project Brief: SagaCode Studio Portfolio

## 1. Project Vision
**SagaCode Studio** is an independent development lab and "One-Person Company" dedicated to high-density quality in Indie Games, Frontend Design, and AI Solutions. The portfolio serves as a digital headquarters that reflects the philosophy: **"Small but Mighty."**

The goal is to move away from generic "AI-generated" aesthetics (AI slop) in favor of a bespoke, artisanal, and high-performance digital experience that demonstrates technical mastery and artistic vision.

---

## 2. Target Audience
- **Founders & Startups:** Looking for a high-velocity, precision-engineered technical partner.
- **Indie Game Developers:** Seeking custom tooling, shaders, or cross-platform deployment expertise.
- **Tech Enthusiasts:** Interested in the intersection of LLMs, generative agents, and modern frontend architecture.

---

## 3. Brand Identity & Design System
The visual language is rooted in **Modern Minimalism** with a focus on typography and subtle, high-end motion.

- **Theme:** Dark Mode (Modern/Tech)
- **Primary Palette:** Deep Zinc (#09090b) background with vibrant Purple (#a855f7) and Cyan (#22d3ee) accents.
- **Typography:** Inter (San-serif) — focusing on strong visual hierarchy and readability.
- **Vibe:** Professional, futuristic, artisan, and deliberate.

---

## 4. Site Map & Core Features
The website is structured to guide the user through the studio's expertise and proof-of-work.

### A. Home (Animated)
- **Hero:** Shimmering gradient text with a clear "One-Person Advantage" message.
- **Our Craft:** High-level overview of the three core pillars (Games, AI, Frontend).
- **Newsletter:** "Stay in the Loop" for insights.

### B. Services
- **Indie Games:** Custom engines (Unity/Godot), cross-platform, shaders.
- **Frontend Design:** Next.js, Tailwind, performance-first UIs.
- **AI Solutions:** Automation, LLM fine-tuning, RAG integration.

### C. Projects (Portfolio)
- Case-study driven gallery showcasing technical foundation and specific outcomes.
- Includes projects like SagaWeb and AI Lab experiments.

### D. About
- **The Philosophy:** Deep dive into the independent developer model.
- **Values:** High Density, Extreme Ownership, and Future-Proofing.

### E. Blog (Notebook)
- Thought leadership on engineering, design, and business.
- Minimalist reading experience.

### F. Contact
- Direct bridge to the creator via a clean, focused "Start a Project" form.

---

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Framework:** Next.js 14+ (App Router).
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS for rapid, maintainable design tokens.
- **Animations:** Pure CSS-based transitions and subtle Framer Motion (optional) to ensure no performance "jank."
- **Performance:** Aiming for 100/100 Lighthouse scores through SSG/ISR and optimized asset loading.

---

## 6. Success Metrics
- **Conversion:** High quality-to-contact ratio (form submissions).
- **Brand Perception:** Positive feedback on the unique "artisanal" design vs. generic templates.
- **Engagement:** Increased time-on-site through the Blog/Notebook content.
